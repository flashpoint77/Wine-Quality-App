«Wine is the healthiest and most hygienic of beverages.» Louis Pasteur

The wine industry has shown significant growth in recent decades, which is why new technologies have been applied in order to support this growth both for the production and sale of products.

Raise a glass!


During the production stage, various factors can be modified such as: acidity, sugar concentration, temperature, yeast species, presence of air. Combinations of these conditions can lead to the production of lower or higher quality wines. It should be borne in mind that the quality of a wine directly influences its value, so classifying different batches or vintages is an important task when marketing the product. Likewise, the monitoring of physicochemical properties is a vitally important factor when making wines and its understanding can help in the production of better quality wines.
